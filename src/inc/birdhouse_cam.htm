Bluebirds are on the endangered species list, they have a lot of competition and dangers trying to survive in suburban landscapes. This project serves a few purposes:

  • Provide a safe nesting house for Bluebirds
  • Educational value in being able to witness nest building, egg sitting, hatching and rearing processes
  • Easy way to care for birds and intervening against any predators

Over the span of a couple years, I created 3 different versions of this project. How one makes something like this varies depending on the conditions of the site. In my case:

  • Ideal location about 20 feet from my house
  • Nearby wall of house had outdoor electrical outlet
  • I wasn't interested in putting a hole in the house and trying to run ethernet to the house

This means that running on battery/solar (and the challenges involved) wasn't a requirement, but using WIFI for connectivity was.
