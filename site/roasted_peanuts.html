Roasted Peanuts

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350

Place unshelled peanuts flat on baking sheet

Bake for 15 mins, stir.

Bake 5 mins, stir. Repeat if not done

Dust lightly with popcorn salt (very fine)

Let cool completely before storing
