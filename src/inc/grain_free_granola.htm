Grain Free

Yummy for breakfast or snack

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup unsweetened dried coconut flakes
  • 2/3 cup raw cashews
  • 2/3 cup pecan pieces
  • 2/3 cup hulled sunflower seeds
  • 2/3 cup raw pumpkin seeds
  • 2/3 cup raw sesame seeds
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
